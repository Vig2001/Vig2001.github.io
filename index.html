Biography

Welcome to my page! 👋🏾 I am a PhD student at UCL, specializing in statistics. 🤓📊📈 My research focuses on developing new methods and theory for benchmarking observational studies against randomised controlled trials (RCTs) and so is closely related to generalisability, data fusion (trial augmentation), and sensitivity analysis. I am passionate about exploring problems related to covariate shift. E.g. What happens when the target population is different from the source? Can we bound the bias arising from varying levels of covariate shift? Is it possible to create methods robust to this shift? 🤔

With the recent advancements and growing interest in AI, I am also interested in how my research could inform robust and generalisable predictive models! 🤖

Prior to my PhD, I had a very brief stint working as an actuary and then as a data scientist in marketing mix modelling. In my free time I enjoy all things cricket, I am currently trying to get back into playing the piano regularly, and am thinking about taking lessons on (Japanese) Jiu Jitsu! 🏏🎹🥋
